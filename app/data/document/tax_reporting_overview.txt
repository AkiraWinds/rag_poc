Tax Reporting Overview for Freelancers and Small Businesses

Section 1: Introduction

The platform provides tools to help freelancers and small businesses manage their tax obligations.
Tax-related data is automatically collected from invoices, expenses, and payments recorded in the system.
This information can be used to generate tax summaries and reports.

Section 2: Supported Tax Reports

Users can generate the following tax-related reports:
- Annual income summary
- Expense breakdown by category
- VAT summary (for applicable regions)
- Vendor payment summaries for tax filing

These reports are designed to support common tax filing workflows but do not replace professional tax advice.

Section 3: Generating a Tax Summary Report

To generate a tax summary report:

1. Navigate to the Reports section.
2. Select Tax Reports.
3. Choose the reporting period.
4. Click Generate Report.

The system will compile income and expense data for the selected period and present it in a downloadable format.

Section 4: Exporting Tax Data

Tax reports can be exported in PDF or CSV format.
Exported data can be shared with accountants or imported into external tax filing tools.
Users are responsible for ensuring the accuracy of exported data before submitting it to tax authorities.

Section 5: Disclaimer

The platform provides tax-related information for informational purposes only.
Users should consult a qualified tax professional to ensure compliance with local regulations.
